Vision

To bring out a generation of energetic, resourceful and formally educated individuals who have acquired skill sets that match modern requirements.

Who We Are

Unleashing Infinite Potential Since 1993

Every individual is blessed with infinite potential. An ocean of abilities brims within us. We only need to open ourselves to new knowledge and practices to unleash our abilities and achieve our life goals.

The institution inspires students to think creatively, explore the lesser known realms of life and work for social empowerment. Apart from classroom tutoring, we identify and nourish unique talents in students.

Integrated Learning Program

A comprehensive, five year course that combines Islamic studies with secular streams of knowledge. The program has been bifurcated into Senior Secondary and Graduation levels, partnering with NIOS and IGNOU to provide a prestigious Islamic degree alongside standard certification.

WHY
GREEN VALLEY ACADEMY?

VISION

To bring out a generation of energetic, resourceful and formally educated individuals who have acquired skill sets that match their inborn traits and strengths, and who shall willingly take up leadership roles in community development with the highest levels of integrity.

CAMPUS

Green Valley Academy is nestled in a verdant hillside in Manjeri, Malappuram. The serene campus which encompasses the main block of the institution along with the hostels, playgrounds and the auditorium is spread out in an area of around 4 acres. It provides students a conducive atmosphere for learning, away from the bustle of city life.

MANAGEMENT

The institution is professionally managed by a seven member educational and charitable trust named Green Valley Foundation Trust. The trustee members as well as the top management aim for achieving the highest educational standards to transform the institution into a Center of Excellence.

Our Programs

Programmes Designed
for Every Learner

Two flagship programs built to develop knowledge, character and capability across different age groups and learning journeys.

5 Years Program

ILP

Integrated Learning Programme

The Integrated Learning Program (ILP) is a comprehensive 5-year residential curriculum that blends academic rigor with Islamic values, personal development, and vocational skills. Students emerge as grounded, well-rounded individuals prepared to contribute meaningfully to society.

Each year focuses on a progressive layer of learning — from foundational literacy and numeracy through to advanced research, leadership, and community engagement. The program balances classroom learning with real-world exposure.

  • Full-time residential 5-year structured curriculum
  • Academic + Islamic studies + life skills integration
  • Personalised mentorship and progress tracking

3 Years Program

ILS

Islamic Life Solutions

Islamic Life Solutions (ILS) is a focused 3-year program designed to equip students with the knowledge, values, and practical tools rooted in Islamic principles. It prepares graduates to navigate modern challenges while staying anchored in their faith and identity.

The program blends Quranic studies, Fiqh, Islamic history, Arabic language, and contemporary life skills — producing graduates who are confident, ethical, and community-ready through a blend of classroom instruction and experiential learning.

  • Quran, Fiqh, Hadith and Arabic language curriculum
  • Contemporary life skills and ethical grounding
  • Community engagement and leadership development

ACADEMY COURSES &
VOCATIONAL COURSES

View all

Higher Secondary

We offer Higher Secondary courses under the syllabus prescribed by the Kerala Board of Public Examinations. Our institution provides two academic streams — Humanities and Commerce — designed to deliver a strong foundation in higher secondary education. Along with the prescribed academic curriculum. we focus on the overall intellectual and personal development of students through quality teaching and comprehensive learning support.

3 Year Degree

We offer 3-year degree courses under Sreenarayanaguru Open University. The available programs include B.A. Sociology, B.Com, and BCA, designed to provide students with quality higher education and career-oriented learning opportunities. Along with the prescribed academic syllabus, we ensure comprehensive guidance and support to help students achieve academic excellence and personal growth.

Vocational Courses

We offer a range of vocational courses designed to develop practical skills and enhance career opportunities. Our programs include Diploma in Computer Applications, Electrical Technician, Certificate in Fashion Designing, and other industry-oriented courses. These programs focus on both theoretical knowledge and hands-on training to equip students with the skills required for today’s professional environment.

ADMISSION PROCESS

Three clear steps stand between you and a seat at GreenValley Academy. Here's what the journey looks like.

STEP ONE

PRIMARY EVALUATION
TEST

Begin your journey by submitting a comprehensive digital dossier detailing your academic history, extracurricular pursuits, and a personal statement of intent. Shortlisted candidates will be invited to sit the Primary Evaluation Test at a designated center.

STEP TWO

SELECTION CAMP &
INTERVIEW

Selected candidates are invited for a curated dialogue with our faculty panel. This is a moment to discuss your aspirations, demonstrate your intellectual curiosity, and showcase the qualities that make you a future GreenValley scholar.

STEP THREE

FINAL
SELECTION

The Admissions Committee conducts a holistic review of all elements before extending an invitation to join the incoming cohort of GreenValley scholars. Successful candidates receive their offer letter and onboarding details by post and email.

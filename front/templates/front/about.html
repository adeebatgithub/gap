ABOUT US

A campus built to grow
skill, confidence, and
character.

GreenValley Academy in Manjeri is designed around practical learning, supportive mentorship, and a clear path from classroom study to real opportunity. We nurture every student's potential and prepare them to thrive beyond academics.

Every individual is blessed with infinite potential. An ocean of abilities brims within us. We only need to open ourselves to new knowledge and practices to unleash our abilities and achieve our life goals. Green Valley Academy is an educational institution established in 1993, with the aim of shaping a generation of young, multi-talented individuals who can bring about a social and cultural renaissance. The institution inspires students to think creatively, explore the lesser known realms of life and work for social empowerment. Apart from classroom tutoring, the institution identifies and nourishes unique talents in students and develops them into outstanding skill sets.

Here we also provide ample opportunities for students to interact with intellectuals and thought leaders of various hues. Diligently organized seminars, symposia and brainstorming sessions also widen the horizons of knowledge among students. The institution also lays emphasis on promoting artistic and sporting talent among students with the annually organized art festivals and sports events. To put it simply, Green Valley Academy stands apart from other institutions by shaping individuals as dynamic go-getters who can take on the various challenges of life with determination, integrity and a firm moral footing.

The faculty of the institution is a highly efficient force spearheaded by the Principal Dr. Ashraf Kalpetta, who has authored over 20 books and is an academician of the highest order. He is assisted by a Vice Principal, Administrator and Teaching staff.

The teachers are a young and energetic group, always eager to identify and nurture the skills of students. They are subject experts willing to clarify any doubts raised by students and strive to be friendly mentors. In other words, at the institution no student-teacher gap exists, but only harmonious relationships built on mutual respect and shared purpose.

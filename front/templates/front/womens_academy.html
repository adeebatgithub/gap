ISLAMIC LIFE SOLUTIONS

Green Valley Women's Academy, a new venture with novel ideas, is on the threshold of blooming. It aims at fostering the Z and Alpha generations of girls on the foundations of modern as well as Islamic teachings. The Academy is determined to inculcate them a plethora of subjects that may illuminate their familial, societal, political, economic, and health domains. Graduates of this academy will be equipped with knowledge, culture and sense of responsibility towards empowering the society.

The academy offers a multifaced curriculum for students who have successfully completed any stream of higher secondary course. Comprehensive Islamic studies, skill development workshops, various vocational training and martial arts are intertwined with undergraduate studies.

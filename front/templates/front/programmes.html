AL-SHA’BI

Green Valley Academy has decided to confer the title AL-SHA’BI on those who complete the graduation from the Academy. The word Al- Sha’bi has been derived from the Arabic root word SHA’B which means PEOPLE or SOCIETY. Sha’bi is a Person who represents ordinary people and liked by a lot of people or a person chosen or appointed to act or speak for others. Green valley Academy thinks that it is the most well-matched title for our graduates because living among the people acting and speaking for them is the real mission of our graduates This title realizes and fulfills the long-cherished goal set of Green Valley Academy. The Academy envisages that it has to mould a new generation equipped with profound understanding in Islamic knowledge and has to make them proficient in dealing with contemporary social issues. The Academy has exerted mammoth efforts to bring up enthusiastic and energetic scholars who will grow to be vanguards of community empowerment in future. We are sure that graduates of the Academy will perform well for the well-being and welfare of the society as their title Sha’bi connotes and they will be able to play a lead role in Muslim community steering the people into the right direction.
